Geography

Nature at its finest.

Located in Alberta, Canada, Jasper has many prime locations to experience and enjoy nature. Take to new heights along the many mountain trails throughout the park. Enjoy a dip in one of the lakes or rivers, or ride along them and enjoy the crystalline-colored beauty of the water. Learn of the magnificent history of the geography, the rivers which carve the rocks, or the glaciers atop the mountains. The various biomes and vast landscapes will leave you truly awestruck.
